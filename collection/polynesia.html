Polynesia

Art as Genealogical Inscription.

AustraliaMelanesiaMicronesiaPolynesiaIsland SE Asia

Polynesia is a coherent cultural sphere of chiefly hierarchy, mana, and tapu, where art is genealogical inscription. From Samoan siapo and ‘ava ceremony to Tongan ngatu and the taonga of Aotearoa — the tiki, the toki, the pounamu blade — each object is an active, living entity possessing its own agency.
