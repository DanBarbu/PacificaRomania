Island Southeast Asia

Guardians of the Threshold.

Australia Melanesia Micronesia Polynesia Island SE Asia

Beyond the Pacific proper, the collection extends into Island Southeast Asia — Borneo, Sumatra, the Malay Peninsula, and Myanmar — where the same Eliadean grammar of threshold, ancestor, and sacred centre recurs in carved hardwood, buffalo horn, and cast bronze. Each object is explored at length in the Journal.
