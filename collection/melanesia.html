Melanesia

The Great Engine of Ritual Invention.

AustraliaMelanesiaMicronesiaPolynesiaIsland SE Asia

Melanesia embodies ancestral agency in masks, spirit houses, and grade figures. From the sacred artery of the Sepik River to the war canoes of the Solomon Sea, the slit gongs of Ambrym, and the kava ceremonies of Fiji, the object is never a symbol but an activated presence — a hierophany danced, sounded, and sailed.
