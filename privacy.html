Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026. This policy explains how personal data is processed on pacificaromania.space in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679, “GDPR”), the ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC, and Romanian Law no. 190/2018 and Law no. 506/2004.

1. Who is responsible (data controller)

The controller for this website is the PacificaRomania collection, operated by AlgorithmIntelligence SRL, Casa14, 14–18 Prevederii St, Sector 3, Bucharest, Romania. Contact: danbarbu22@gmail.com. A data protection officer is not required and has not been appointed; enquiries reach the controller directly at that address.

2. What we collect, why, and the legal basis

  • Audience analytics (Matomo) — only if you consent. We record pages viewed, referring site, approximate location (country/region), device and browser type, timestamps, and your IP address in anonymised form. Legal basis: your consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR; cookies are stored under Art. 5(3) ePrivacy with your prior consent. Analytics is self-hosted (Matomo) — data is not shared with any advertising network.
  • Correspondence — if you email us, we process your address and message to reply. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in answering you, Art. 6(1)(f), or steps at your request, Art. 6(1)(b).
  • Hosting & security logs — the site is served by GitHub Pages, which may process your IP address and request metadata to deliver pages and protect against abuse. Legal basis: legitimate interest in security, Art. 6(1)(f).

We do not sell personal data, run advertising trackers, build profiles, or carry out automated decision-making.

4. How long we keep data

Matomo raw visit logs are retained for a limited period (target: 14 months) and then deleted or aggregated. Emails are kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry. Hosting logs follow the provider’s own retention.

5. Who receives the data (processors & transfers)

Hosting: GitHub, Inc. (GitHub Pages). The analytics server is self-hosted; we recommend and intend to keep it within the EU/EEA. Where a provider is located outside the EEA (e.g. GitHub in the United States), transfers rely on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and may object to processing based on legitimate interest, and withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing. To exercise any right, email danbarbu22@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Romanian supervisory authority, ANSPDCP (dataprotection.ro), or your local authority.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date shows the current version.
